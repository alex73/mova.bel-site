NOUNS

 In Belarusian there are several types of declension:

i-stem – feminine

consonantal stem – neuter

a-stem – mostly feminine

o-stem – masculine and neuter

After the numerals два ‘two’, тры ‘three’, чатыры ‘four’ the so-called non-true dual forms for neuter nouns are used: два вяслы, плячы, вакны ‘windows’ (cf. Nom. plur.: вёслы, плечы, вокны).

For feminine nouns the non-true dual forms coincide in form with the Gen.sing.: дзве трубы ‘two pipes’ (сf.Gen.sing. трубы, Nom.plur. трубы).
